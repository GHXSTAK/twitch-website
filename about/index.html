Music

If you've been hanging out on my stream you might've recognized a few tracks that went viral online - like "Отменяй" by REDCHINAWAVE, that song among the others I play belongs to an underground subgenre of electronic music called Witch House.
Witch House blends elements of trap, and ambient music to create a dark and ethereal atmosphere. I emerged in the late 2000s You can read more about its history here.
